About Aabha

Latha Master Beautician

The Artist Behind Aabha

Passion Meets Precision

Aabha By Latha was born from a deep love for beauty and an unwavering commitment to making every woman feel her most radiant self.

Since 2008, Latha has been transforming clients in Kochi with her expert touch, keen eye for detail, and genuine warmth. What began as a single beauty parlour on Shobha Road has grown into one of the most trusted beauty destinations in Vennala and Palarivattam.

Specialising in bridal makeup, hair styling, and holistic skin care, Latha brings decades of experience to every appointment. Her philosophy is simple: every woman deserves to feel beautiful, and that beauty should be as unique as she is.

"All types of beauty services available here — crafted with care, love, and artistry."

— Latha, Founder
